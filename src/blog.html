Things you could do with APIs

8:32pm 03-11-2016

Rudimentary mind control - Scrape youtube comments then filter them by their emotional content using a sentiment analysis API. These comments could then be read to the user, whose mood should shift to match the sentiments expressed.

Backwords - Gather random words, using a random word API. Find the longest possible word that could be made with the letters of said word, excluding the original word. Present this second shorter word to the user, who then must attempt to intuit the original longer word.

Auto Dadactic - Feed any image into Microsoft's Computer Vision API. Appreciate the procedurally generated surrealist poetry that will be returned.
